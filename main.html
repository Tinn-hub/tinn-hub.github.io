Things to Do

Downtown Riverside

Downtown Riverside, CA, is a vibrant and historic area known for its rich cultural heritage and charming architecture. At its heart lies the Mission Inn Hotel & Spa, a stunning example of Mission Revival style that draws visitors from near and far. The area boasts a variety of attractions, including museums, galleries, and lively street festivals, reflecting the city's dynamic arts scene. With its tree-lined streets, diverse dining options, and inviting public spaces, downtown Riverside offers a delightful blend of old-world charm and modern amenities.

Griffin Observatory

Griffith Observatory in Los Angeles is a premier landmark offering breathtaking views of the city and the iconic Hollywood Sign. Situated on the southern slope of Mount Hollywood in Griffith Park, the observatory is a popular destination for both locals and tourists. It features fascinating exhibits on space and science, a high-powered telescope for public use, and the Samuel Oschin Planetarium, known for its captivating shows. Opened in 1935, Griffith Observatory combines education with awe-inspiring vistas, making it a must-visit spot in LA for astronomy enthusiasts and casual visitors alike.

The Board Museum

The Board in Los Angeles is a modern and dynamic urban park located in the heart of the city, known for its vibrant atmosphere and diverse recreational activities. This public space is a hub for skateboarding, biking, and other outdoor sports, attracting both amateur and professional athletes. With its state-of-the-art facilities, The Board offers a unique blend of open green spaces, innovative design, and art installations, creating a lively environment for people of all ages. It serves as a community gathering spot, hosting events, performances, and workshops that celebrate LA's rich cultural diversity and active lifestyle.

Pacific Coast Highway - Freeway 1

The California State Route 1, commonly known as the Pacific Coast Highway (PCH), is one of the most scenic drives in the United States. Stretching along the California coastline from Orange County to Mendocino County, this iconic highway offers breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean, rugged cliffs, and picturesque beaches. Travelers can explore charming coastal towns, visit historic landmarks like Hearst Castle, and enjoy natural wonders such as Big Sur and the Monterey Bay. The PCH is not just a route but a journey that showcases the diverse beauty and culture of California, making it a must-experience for road trip enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Where to Eat

Vanilla Fish

Vanilla Fish in Riverside is a contemporary sushi and Japanese cuisine restaurant renowned for its fresh and innovative dishes. The restaurant offers a diverse menu featuring expertly crafted sushi rolls, sashimi, and other Japanese favorites, all prepared with the highest quality ingredients. With its sleek, modern decor and inviting ambiance, Vanilla Fish provides a chic dining experience perfect for both casual meals and special occasions. Known for its attentive service and creative culinary presentations, Vanilla Fish has quickly become a favorite destination for sushi lovers in the Riverside area.

All That 'N Dim Sum

All That 'n Dim Sum in Rowland Heights is a popular eatery celebrated for its authentic and delicious Cantonese dim sum. This vibrant restaurant offers a wide array of traditional dumplings, buns, and small plates, all freshly made and served in a lively, welcoming atmosphere. Known for its quality ingredients and attentive service, All That 'n Dim Sum has become a favorite spot for both locals and visitors seeking a true taste of Hong Kong-style cuisine. Whether you're craving classic shrimp dumplings or adventurous dishes like chicken feet, this restaurant promises a delightful culinary experience.

Asian Night Market at Phuoc Loc Tho

The Asian Night Market at Phuoc Loc Tho, also known as the "Asian Garden Mall" in Little Saigon, is a vibrant and bustling event that celebrates Asian culture and cuisine. Located in Westminster, CA, this night market offers a diverse array of food stalls featuring authentic dishes from various Asian cultures, including Vietnamese, Thai, and Chinese cuisines. Visitors can enjoy an assortment of street foods, snacks, and desserts while exploring unique shops and entertainment options. The market's lively atmosphere, combined with its rich cultural offerings, makes it a popular destination for food enthusiasts and those looking to experience the dynamic flavors and traditions of Asia.

Pho Holic
