    About Onwer

    The owner of this website

    Philip Saba

    I come from Jordan, I am a composite and editorial photographer, I work with black and white images, Coloring them, recreating the seen building it and remodeling the time frame that was set in an image to bring it back in a modern set of colors and to bring it of the the hazy effect to a sharp image. I also work with composite photography. It's more or less CGI but in a still format with no motion. I also have wedding bundles, different prices, qualities, prints, etc. I also rent out handcrafted jewelry/semi precious jewelry, or sell them to customers in some cases.
